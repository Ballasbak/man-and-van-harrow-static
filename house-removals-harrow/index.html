What "House Removals" actually means

One quote covers the whole job. Movers price the move as a single number that covers crew, vehicles, loading, transport and unloading. No clock-watching.

Best for 2-bed homes and larger. From 2-bed flats to 5-bed family homes. Whole-job pricing is usually cheaper than hourly once you pass roughly two van-loads of belongings.

Includes the things hourly quotes don't. Survey of the move (often by photo or video), heavier-duty crew, suitable vehicles, and protective materials.

Local detail

Survey first for bigger homes. For 3-bed+ Harrow homes, movers will usually want a quick photo or video survey before quoting properly. This avoids "estimated" quotes turning into surprise bills on the day.

Parking matters more than people think. Larger Luton vans and 7.5-tonne lorries can't always get close to properties in older Harrow streets (parts of Pinner, Harrow on the Hill, central Harrow). Movers may price in a longer carry or a smaller shuttle vehicle.

Out-of-London moves. Moves out of Harrow to commuter areas (Chiltern line towns, M25 belt, the south coast) are common. These are priced as whole-job removals because the time-on-road element makes hourly pricing unpredictable.

Common questions

How is House Removals priced?
As a single whole-job quote covering crew, vehicles, loading, transport and unloading.
Do I need a survey?
For 3-bed+ moves, most Harrow movers will want a quick photo/video survey to firm up the quote.
Is packing included?
Usually quoted separately. You can choose full pack, part-pack (kitchen + breakables), or pack yourself.
How far in advance should I book?
2–4 weeks for typical Harrow moves; longer for end-of-month summer weekends.
What if I'm moving out of London?
Whole-job pricing still applies and is normally cheaper than hourly for longer journeys.
