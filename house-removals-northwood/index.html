What is a house removal?

A house removal refers to a full home move, typically involving multiple rooms, larger furniture, and higher overall volume than smaller or single-item jobs.

House removals usually require a larger vehicle or multiple vehicles, along with one or more movers to safely load, transport, and unload your belongings. Depending on the service level chosen, movers may also assist with packing materials, furniture dismantling and reassembly, and the handling of heavy or delicate items. See How It Works.
