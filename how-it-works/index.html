What is MAZE?

MAZE is a UK quote comparison platform for moving companies. It is not a removals company itself. When you click Compare quotes, your move details are sent to MAZE, which collects live quotes from available local movers and shows them back to you side by side. You choose who to book.

We use MAZE so you don't have to ring round movers, fill in multiple forms, or get cold-called by sales teams. One enquiry, multiple quotes, your choice.

What it costs you

Comparing quotes is free. There is no booking fee from this site or from MAZE. You only pay the mover you choose to book.

Common questions

Who actually does the move?
The local mover you pick on MAZE. They quote, they turn up, you pay them.
How quickly do quotes come back?
Usually within minutes for Man & Van and small moves; longer for big house removals that need a survey.
Can I edit my move after submitting?
Yes — you can update details with the mover before booking.
What happens to my data?
It's used to match you to movers and contact you about the quote. See our privacy policy.
Is MAZE only for Harrow?
No — MAZE is a UK-wide comparison platform. This site focuses on Harrow because that's where we know the local market.
