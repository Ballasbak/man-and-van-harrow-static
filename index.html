Common questions

What is MAZE and why am I being sent there?
MAZE is a UK comparison platform for moving companies. We pass your move details to MAZE, which collects quotes from available local movers and shows them to you side by side. You stay in control of who you book.
Is the comparison free?
Yes. Getting and comparing quotes is free and there's no obligation to book.
Do I have to take phone calls?
No. Quotes are sent through MAZE and you choose whether to contact a mover.
How fast can someone come?
Same-day and next-day availability is common for Man & Van moves in Harrow. Larger house removals usually need a few days' notice.
Do you cover my area?
We cover the whole Harrow district and the surrounding HA, NW, UB and West London postcodes. See our areas page.
