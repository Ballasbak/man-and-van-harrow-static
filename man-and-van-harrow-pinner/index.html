Pinner local notes

Station, High Street and parking access

Station access: Pinner Underground station is on the Metropolitan line. Around the station and Bridge Street, agree a practical collection point if loading directly outside is restricted.

Older streets: Pinner's historic High Street and nearby roads can have tighter access than newer residential streets. Share vehicle-access and carrying-distance details before the mover quotes.
