What actually changes the price

  • Volume of belongings — the single biggest driver. A full 2-bed with a garage costs more than an empty 2-bed.
  • Access at both ends — stairs, lift, parking distance, narrow streets in older Harrow conservation areas.
  • Date — month-end and the last Friday of the month are peak; midweek and mid-month are cheaper.
  • Distance — local Harrow moves are cheapest; out-of-London moves add transport time.
  • Add-ons — packing service, materials, dismantling, piano/safe handling, storage.

How to keep the price down

  • Get quotes 2–3 weeks ahead so you're not paying for urgency.
  • Avoid the last Friday of the month if you can.
  • Pack yourself where possible — packing service is usually £200–£600 extra.
  • Be honest about volume — under-quoted moves turn into over-priced ones on the day.

Common questions

Are these prices fixed?
No. They are typical guide prices for budgeting. Actual quotes come from local movers through MAZE.
Why is hourly sometimes cheaper than whole-job?
For small loads, hourly wins because the job is short. For bigger loads, whole-job wins because the mover doesn't price in worst-case time.
Are these prices for weekdays or weekends?
Mid-month weekdays. Expect 10–20% more on month-end Fridays and Saturdays.
Do quotes include VAT?
Most do, but always check — confirm with the mover.
Does MAZE charge me?
No. You pay the mover you choose. MAZE is the comparison platform.
