🧮 Man & Van Costs in Harrow (2026)

For small moves such as:

  • Studio flats
  • Student moves
  • Single-room relocations
  • Furniture transport

You can expect:

£45 – £70 per hour (1 man + small van) £65 – £95 per hour (2 men + Luton van)

Minimum booking is usually 2 hours.

So most small local moves cost:

£120 – £280 total

Prices increase if:

  • There are stairs without a lift
  • Long walking distance to the van
  • Congestion or parking restrictions
  • Heavy items (pianos, safes)

🏢 Small Flat Move Costs (1–2 Bedroom)

For a 1 or 2 bed flat within Harrow:

Expect:

👉 £250 – £600

Depends on:

  • Floor level
  • Lift availability
  • Packing requirements
  • Travel distance

A typical 1-bed flat locally might take 3–5 hours.

🏠 3 Bedroom House Removal Costs in Harrow

For larger properties:

👉 £600 – £1,200+

This usually includes:

  • 2–3 movers
  • Large Luton van
  • Basic furniture protection
  • Loading and unloading

Full packing services can add:

👉 £200 – £400 extra

Long-distance moves (outside London) increase total cost.

🚫 Hidden Charges to Watch For

Some movers may add:

  • Extra for stairs
  • Extra for congestion charge
  • Parking permit fees
  • Last-minute booking fees
  • Dismantling/reassembly charges
  • Waiting time

Always confirm what’s included before agreeing.

A trustworthy mover will be transparent.

📍 Harrow-Specific Factors That Affect Price

Harrow has:

  • Controlled parking zones
  • Narrow residential streets
  • Flat conversions
  • Permit restrictions

These can increase move time.

Planning parking in advance can reduce costs significantly.

You can check Harrow Council parking rules before your move.

💡 How to Reduce Your Moving Costs

  • Move midweek (cheaper than weekends)
  • Avoid end-of-month dates
  • Declutter before moving
  • Pack yourself (if possible)
  • Be flexible with time slots

Even small changes can reduce the final bill.
