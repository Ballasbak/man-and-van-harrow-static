How it works

From Harrow quote request to your partner portal

MAZE is a UK comparison and lead-distribution platform, not a removals company. This Harrow website is a feeder page that sends prospective partners to MAZE.

1

A customer requests quotes

Customers provide their contact details, pickup postcode, move date and service requirements through a quote engine.

2

MAZE matches eligible partners

Qualified requests are matched by pickup postcode prefix, service type, availability, preferences and credit balance.

3

The lead reaches your portal

Qualified leads are sent by email and shown in the MAZE partner portal. You contact the customer directly and agree your own price and terms.

Partner controls

Choose the leads that fit your operation

Partners control intake before distribution through settings in the MAZE portal.

Coverage and services

Choose exact postcode prefixes such as HA1, HA2, HA3, HA7, HA8 and HA9. Offer Man & Van, Removals, or both.

Timing and capacity

Set your minimum lead time, daily cap, long-distance preference and whether to consider suitable requests just outside your area.

Pause when needed

Use holiday mode for a date range, or pause and resume leads while keeping your coverage, preferences and credits intact.

Prepaid credits

How MAZE partner credits work

Partners top up prepaid credits through Stripe in the MAZE portal. Credits are deducted only when a qualified paid_quote lead is distributed to the partner. Free enquiries are not distributed to partners and are never charged.

Default starter pricing

The default Band C cost is 5 credits for a Removals lead and 3 credits for a Man & Van lead. Higher-demand postcode prefixes may use a different band.

Your credit price

The price of each credit can vary by partner. Your portal shows your rate, available bundles and exact lead cost before you top up.

If your balance is below the cost of a matching lead, MAZE skips your account until you top up. There is no overdraft, invoice or pay-later option.

Lead allocation

Qualified leads are shared, not exclusive

Between 1 and 6 vetted partners can receive the same matching qualified lead. There is no accept/reject step after delivery.

Matched upstream

MAZE checks postcode-prefix coverage, active status, service type, distance preference, lead time, capacity and credits before distribution.

Sent immediately

The partner receives customer details by email and in the portal when the qualified lead is distributed.

You run the booking

Partners contact the customer directly and agree their own pricing, availability, booking terms and service delivery.

Common questions

MAZE partner FAQs

How many partners receive each lead?
Between 1 and 6, depending on the postcode and matching preferences. There's no accept/reject step - when a lead is sent to you, it's yours to contact.
What does a Harrow lead cost?
Most HA-postcode leads sit on the default Band C: 5 credits for a Removals lead and 3 credits for a Man & Van lead. High-demand prefixes can sit on a higher band - you see the exact cost per area in your MAZE portal.
How much is a credit?
Price per credit is set per partner in pence. You'll see your rate and any bundle discounts in the portal before topping up.
What happens if I run out of credits?
If your balance is below the cost of a matching lead, you're skipped and the lead goes to the next eligible partner. Top up to resume.
Are the leads exclusive to me?
No. MAZE is a comparison platform. Up to 6 vetted movers can receive the same lead - speed of response matters.
Do I pay for unqualified enquiries?
No. Only fully qualified paid_quote leads with contact details are charged. Rough-estimate enquiries are free.
Can I pause without losing my account?
Yes. Use Holiday Mode for a date range, or set your daily cap to zero. Areas, settings and credits stay intact.
Do I need to cover all of Harrow?
No. You pick the exact UK postcode prefixes, such as HA1, HA2 and HA8, that you want. You can add neighbouring prefixes too, or opt in to leads just outside your area.
