What counts as a small move

  • A single item (sofa, mattress, fridge, bike).
  • A few boxes between addresses.
  • A studio or very small 1-bed.
  • A part-load — you only fill half a van.

Common questions

Can you collect from a shop or warehouse?
Yes — list the pickup as a commercial address in the move details.
Do you wrap and protect items?
Most movers include basic blankets and straps. Specialist wrapping for fragile items is usually an add-on.
Is there a minimum charge?
Yes — typically a 1–2 hour minimum even for single items.
Can I track the mover?
The mover you book will share contact details directly once the job is confirmed through MAZE.
