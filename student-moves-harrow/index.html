What student moves usually look like

Single room or studio. A few suitcases, bedding, a desk setup, a bike. Typically 1 van + 1–2 crew.

End-of-term / end-of-tenancy timing. Late June and late September are peak — book a few days ahead.

Often short distance. Room-to-room within Harrow on the Hill, between Harrow and central London halls, or back to a family home in the home counties.
