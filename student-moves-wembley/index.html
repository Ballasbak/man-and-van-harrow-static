What Is a Student Move?

Student moves are ideal for university students, shared accommodation, studio flats, and short-term rentals. They typically involve smaller loads, flexible dates, and quicker turnaround times compared to full house moves.

Student moves are common when relocating between term-time accommodation, moving into halls, changing flat shares, or transporting furniture and personal belongings at the start or end of the academic year. With Man and Van Wembley, you can compare suitable movers for your student move.
